If the entirety of the order is not received and processed by our fulfilment center within 60 days since the order date, we will not issue your refund. In this event any goods received will not be returned to you.

B) Along with your returned order you must clearly include details regarding your order, including (1) Your ClickBank Order ID (found in your order receipt), (2) Your Full Name, (3) Your Full Address where the shipment was received, (4) Your Email Address and (5) your Phone Number so that a member of our fulfilment center staff can clearly read and process your return. Contact our support team at contact@glucoclear.com if you need help retrieving this information.
